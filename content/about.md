**Iris Hallberg** is a writer and editor based in a small apartment that overlooks a
quiet street. She keeps this blog as a working notebook — essays that are mostly
finished, notes that mostly aren't, occasional technical pieces when the mood strikes,
and links to other people's work that she wishes more people had read.

This site is called **Lantern**. It is set in **Fraunces** (display) and
**Source Serif 4** (body), with **JetBrains Mono** for code. Built with Hugo, hosted
on a small static host, designed to be read slowly.

Write to her at `iris [at] example [dot] com`. She reads everything, and replies when
she can.

## Colophon

This site runs on a Hugo theme called *Lantern* — three home layouts (column,
broadsheet, index), automatic dark mode with a manual override, OKLCH accents, and a
reading width you can tune in config. The source is open; see the repository for
details.
